WALKING MEDITATIONS
Tell Me

For what
purpose
or reason.

Are there;

ups and downs,
whys and wherefores,
does and don’ts,
joys and sorrows,
successes and failures,
gains and losses,
likes and dislikes,
highs and lows,
loves and hates,
rights and wrongs,
yes’es and no’s

why,
tell me
why?

To what
purpose
Or reason?

Do we see and hear
look and listen
touch and taste
feel and don't feel
think and don't think
speak and don't speak

is there a why
or no
why?

Uruguay 2007
