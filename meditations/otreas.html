WALKING MEDITATIONS
The Treasures Of The Heart

Where is your wealth
they ask?

Within
deep within
I say;

And it grows each
passing day
in every
way.

It cannot
be saved,
sold
nor taken.
only given away
each
and every day.

What is this wealth
they ask?

More precious
more dear
than all the wealth.

Known,
or unknown.

Listen
and I will
tell you
what
you ask?

These
are a few
of my
coins
of living gold;

the love
of loving

the joy
of enjoying

the happiness
of happy

the thinking
of thinking

the feel
of feeling

the touch
of touching

the taste
of tasting

the smell
of smelling

the hearing
of seeing

the seeing
of hearing

the newness
of new

the nearness
of near

the mercifulness
of mercy

the faithfulness
of faith

the truthfulness
of truth

the beautifulness
of beauty

the desire
of desiring

the good
of goodness

the right
of right-ness

the brave 
of braveness

the great
of greatness

the care
of caring

the laughter
of laughing

the wondering
of wonder

the expectation
of expecting

the aliveness
of alive

the I-am-ness
of I am

the beingness
of being

the knowing
of knowing

the silence
of silence

the peacefulness
of peace

the awareness
of awareness

the blissfulness
of bliss

the Oneness
of Oneness

Thailand
2006
